About SIP

Built for Trust.
Designed for Scale.

SIP (Safe ID Promise) is an advanced AI-powered identity verification platform purpose-built for the Indian fintech, insurtech, and gig economy ecosystem. We believe that security and privacy are not trade-offs — they are complementary pillars.

Our platform processes Aadhaar and PAN documents locally using on-device AI models, ensuring that no raw biometric or personal data is ever transmitted to external servers. Decisions are transparent, explainable, and auditable.

  • All biometric data processed in ephemeral memory — zero persistent PII storage
  • Compliant with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP) 2023
  • On-premise deployment available for enterprise clients with air-gapped environments
  • Explainable AI — every decision comes with a human-readable rationale and confidence breakdown
